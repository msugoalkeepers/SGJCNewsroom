Users

Approving New Users

When a student signs in for the first time their account is placed in a pending queue. They see an "Access Pending" screen and cannot enter the newsroom until approved. A badge on the Team nav item shows how many are waiting.

Approve a Pending Student
1
Click Team in the left sidebar. A number badge shows how many are pending.
2
At the top of the Team page find the Pending Approvals section.
3
Choose their Role (Reporter, Section Editor, Producer, Editor) and Section.
4
Click Approve. They are notified immediately and can enter the newsroom.
ℹTo deny a request click Deny. Their pending record is deleted. They can sign in again to resubmit if it was a mistake.
📌The first person to sign in is automatically made Editor-in-Chief with full access. Every subsequent user is pending until approved.

Remove & Reactivate Members

Only the Editor-in-Chief can remove members. Removal is a soft delete — the account is locked immediately but the data and story history are fully preserved.

Removing a Member
  • On the Team page each member card has a red Remove button — visible to EIC only, and not on your own card
  • Two confirmation dialogs appear before removal is committed — you cannot do this by accident
  • The removed user is locked out immediately and receives a notification
  • If they try to open the app they see an "Access Removed" screen
Reactivating a Member
  • A Removed Members section appears at the bottom of the Team page (EIC only)
  • Click ↩ Reactivate to restore them to pending status
  • Then approve them from the Pending Approvals section — assign their role and section as normal
🛡The Remove button does not appear on your own team card — you can never accidentally remove yourself.

Roles & Permissions

Roles control what each team member can see and do. Editors can change reporter and section-editor roles. Only the EIC can change editor-level roles or remove members.

Reporter
Write stories · Submit for review · Submit pitches · Add comments and reactions · See own assignments only (plus any co-assigned)
Cannot: see other reporters’ assignments · create assignments · approve users
Section Editor
All reporter abilities · Assign stories within their section · Review and send back stories within their section
Cannot: review stories outside their section · publish stories
Producer
Build and manage broadcast rundowns · Assign segments · View all rundowns
Cannot: assign print or digital stories · publish stories
Editor
Assign stories to anyone · Review all sections · Approve and deny pending users · Change reporter and section-editor roles
Cannot: publish stories · remove members · change editor-level roles
Editor-in-Chief
Full access — publish stories · unlock published content · change any role · remove and reactivate members · export stories as PDF
No restrictions — full control over all newsroom features

Editor Markup Mode

When a story is In Review, editors and EICs see a ✏ Editor Markup toggle button in the story editor toolbar. Turning it on lets you type changes directly in the story — everything you type appears in red so the reporter can clearly see what changed.

  • An orange banner at the top of the editor confirms markup mode is active
  • The reporter sees red-highlighted text when they open the story
  • Each markup session is saved as a version in Version History
  • The reporter can click ⇄ Compare in the Version History panel to see their original draft next to the edited version side by side
  • Turn markup mode off to return to normal editing
💡Use the Comments panel alongside markup mode — markup shows what changed, comments explain why. Together they give reporters clear, actionable feedback.

Publishing & PDF Export

Only the Editor-in-Chief can publish stories. Once published, a story moves to the Archive and is locked from further editing.

  • Open the story in the Story Editor. When status is In Review the Approve & Publish button appears in the top right.
  • A confirmation dialog appears before publishing is committed
  • The reporter receives a 🎉 notification when their story is published
  • Published stories appear in the Archive — anyone can click to read them in full
  • If a correction is needed after publishing, click Unlock (EIC only) to return it to Draft
Exporting as PDF

Any team member can export a published story as a formatted PDF. Open any published story and click ↧ Export PDF in the toolbar. In the browser print dialog, set the destination to Save as PDF.

Broadcast Rundowns

Producers, Editors, and EICs can create and manage rundowns. Reporters can view rundowns and see their own segments.

Create a Rundown
1
Go to Rundowns in the sidebar. Click + New Rundown.
2
Fill in show name, air date, air time, total duration, and producer notes.
3
Click the show pill to open the rundown. Click + Add Segment for each segment.
4
Fill in segment type (VO, SOT, LIVE, READER, TOSS, BREAK, WEATHER, SPORTS, OPEN/CLOSE), duration, reporter/anchor, intro copy, chyron, video source, audio type, and outcue.
5
Drag rows to reorder. Click any row to view full details in a popup.

Troubleshooting

Most issues can be resolved without any technical intervention. Here are the most common situations and what to do.

What you seeLikely causeWhat to do
User stuck on "Access Pending"Not yet approvedTeam → Pending Approvals → Approve them
User stuck on "Access Removed"Account was deactivatedTeam → Removed Members → click ↩ Reactivate
Team page only shows EICOlder accounts missing an internal status fieldLog in as EIC or Editor — a background fix runs automatically within seconds and all members reappear
Story won’t open ("Failed to load")Connectivity issue or user not fully approvedConfirm user status is "approved" on Team page. Have them refresh and try again.
Grammar check failsNo internet or service temporarily unavailableBrowser spell check (red underlines in editor) still works. Try grammar check again in a few minutes.
"+ New Rundown" not visibleUser role doesn’t include rundown accessGo to Team, find the user, change role to Producer, Editor, or EIC
PDF export opens blank popupBrowser blocking pop-upsAllow pop-ups for the newsroom URL in browser settings, then try again
"In the Newsroom" shows fewer membersPresence dots take up to 30 seconds to appearWait 30–60 seconds. All approved members always show — only the colored dots depend on timing.
Nobody can log in at allA backend configuration issueContact your technical administrator — this cannot be resolved from inside the app.
💻For any issue that persists, press F12 in your browser, click the Console tab, and take a screenshot of any red error messages. Share these with your technical administrator.
